How it works

Three steps.
One experiment at a time.

1

Ask a question

Turn any idea into an experiment. "What happens if I walk 20 minutes before work?" Save ideas to your Parking lot and start when you're ready.

2

Check in daily

Rate your energy Low, Medium, or High. Add a quick note if something stands out. Under 2 minutes. No streaks. No shame if you miss a day.

3

Discover what works

At the end of the experiment, see your patterns. What gave you energy? What drained you? Now you have data - not just a feeling.
