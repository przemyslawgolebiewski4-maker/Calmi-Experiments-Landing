How it works

An experiment is
not a goal.

It is a question. What happens if I try X for a few days? No pressure. Just curiosity. And real data.

1

Ask a question

Turn any idea into an experiment. "What happens if I wake up 30 min earlier?" Save ideas to Parking and start when you are ready.

2

Check in daily

Rate your energy Low / Medium / High and add a quick note. No streaks. No judgment if you miss a day. Every day counts.

Discover what works

After the experiment, reflect on what you found. See patterns in your energy. Build actual knowledge about yourself.
